Capabilities

What the AI looks for

A full analysis pipeline — from raw pixel difference to actionable risk intelligence.

🔍

Pixel-level change detection

Compares grayscale histograms with Gaussian blur and morphological operations to isolate genuine structural changes from noise or lighting differences.

🏗️

YOLO object recognition

YOLOv8n scans the current site image to identify and locate construction equipment, vehicles, personnel, and structures — with confidence scores and bounding boxes.

⚠️

Automated risk scoring

A three-tier risk model (Low, Medium, High) based on changed pixel percentage, region count, and detected activity — giving you an instant site health summary.

🗺️

Change heatmaps & masks

Visual heatmaps highlight activity intensity across the site. Binary masks show exactly which zones changed. Export everything as images, JSON, or CSV for your records.
